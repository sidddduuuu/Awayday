# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Friends coordinating travel to a live event, with one organizer responsible for keeping the group on time without exposing each traveler’s private budget or preferences.

## Product Purpose

Awayday turns a group travel request into one coordinated arrival plan, monitors the journey, and repairs ordinary disruptions before they threaten kickoff.

## Positioning

Awayday owns the group’s arrival outcome instead of returning another list of travel options.

## Brand Personality

Calm, capable mission control for an exciting day out. The product should make complicated travel feel understandable and recoverable.

## Anti-references

Not a crowded booking marketplace, a generic AI chat window, or a dashboard that exposes private traveler constraints.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with group readiness and the current weak link.
2. Show one coordinated plan, not a wall of alternatives.
3. Explain every agent action in practical language.
4. Keep permissions and private constraints visible only to the people who own them.
5. Interrupt the user only when a real rule must bend.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use semantic HTML, full keyboard access, clear focus states, readable contrast, and reduced-motion support. Never communicate trip status by color alone.
